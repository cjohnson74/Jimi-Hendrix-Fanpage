Biography

Jimi Hendrix is considered to be one of the greatest guitarist of all time. An American rock guitarist and singer who is known for fusing a few different types of genres such as: blues, jazz, rock, and soul. He combined these traditions with avant-garde rock to recreate the electric guitar into his image. Born in Seatle November 27, 1942 and died in London, England September 18, 1970 joining the popular "27 club".
